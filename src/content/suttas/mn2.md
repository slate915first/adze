# MN 2 — Sabbāsava Sutta
*All the Taints*

The Buddha describes seven methods for abandoning the āsavas (taints) — chief among them wise attention (yoniso manasikāra): what to attend to, what not to attend to. The foundational sutta on the mechanics of skillful attention.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/right_view|Right view]] — The forerunner of the entire path. Not a belief — a way of seeing. Several complementary doors into it, from several teachers.
- [[subcategories/unwanted_thoughts|Unwanted thoughts]] — Rumination, mental proliferation (papañca), thoughts you cannot stop. The Buddha taught specific methods for this — not "let them go" platitudes.
