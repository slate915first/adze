# SN 12.2 — Vibhaṅga Sutta
*Dependent Origination — Analysis*

The Buddha enumerates the twelve links of dependent origination (paṭiccasamuppāda) one by one with a definition of each. The analytical reference for the teaching that grounds everything else in the dhamma.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/right_view|Right view]] — The forerunner of the entire path. Not a belief — a way of seeing. Several complementary doors into it, from several teachers.
