# MN 27 — Cūḷahatthipadopama Sutta
*The Shorter Elephant-Footprint Simile*

A brahmin asks how far one can conclude the Buddha is awakened from outward signs. The Buddha replies with the elephant-footprint simile: one must follow the whole path — sīla, restraint of the senses, mindfulness, the jhānas, full knowing — before the conclusion is complete. Bodhi's extended teaching on gradual training.
