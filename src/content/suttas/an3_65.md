# AN 3.65 — Kālāma Sutta
*To the Kālāmas*

The Buddha's instruction on right reliance: do not accept teachings on the basis of authority, tradition, or reasoning alone. Test them in your own experience.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/doubt|Doubt]] — When doubt about the teaching or the path arises. The Buddha did not demand faith — he invited testing. The sutta that tells you how to test.
