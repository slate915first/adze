# AN 7.58 — Pacalā Sutta
*Drowsing*

The Buddha to Moggallāna on what to do when overcome by drowsiness — seven progressive remedies, from changing the meditation object to walking with eyes open in any direction.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/family_and_friends|Family & friendship]] — How a householder relates to parents, spouse, children, friends. The Buddha taught a specific code for lay life; it is still useful.
- [[subcategories/metta|Loving-kindness]] — Mettā is not a feeling to summon — it is a quality to cultivate, until it becomes boundless. The suttas that frame what that actually means in practice.
