# SN 47.19 — Sedaka Sutta
*The Acrobat*

The teacher and the apprentice on the bamboo pole — "I will look after you, you look after me." The Buddha replies: "It is not so. Look after yourself, that is how you look after others. Look after others — that is how you look after yourself."
