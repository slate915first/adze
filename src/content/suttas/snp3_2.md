# Sn 3.2 — Padhāna Sutta
*The Striving*

Māra's direct address to the Bodhisatta during his years of effort — naming his ten armies (kāmā, arati, khuppipāsā, taṇhā, thīna-middha, bhīrū, vicikicchā, makkha-thambha, lābha-siloka, att-ukkaṃsana) and the Bodhisatta's reply. The canonical source of the Māra-armies framing this app uses throughout.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/effort|Effort & perseverance]] — When you want to give up. When the practice feels stuck. The Bodhisatta's own encounters with Māra — and how he stayed.
