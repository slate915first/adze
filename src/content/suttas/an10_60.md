# AN 10.60 — Girimānanda Sutta
*To Girimānanda*

Given when Ven. Girimānanda was gravely ill: ten perceptions (saññā) that, when contemplated, can by themselves relieve serious suffering — perception of impermanence, of non-self, of the unattractive, of danger, of abandoning, and five more. The sutta to read when sick, in pain, or facing death.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/grief_and_loss|Grief & loss]] — Illness, aging, loss of a loved one, facing your own death. The suttas the tradition has always given to the bereaved and the dying.
