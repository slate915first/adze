# SN 35.28 — Āditta Sutta
*The Fire Sermon*

The Buddha's third formal discourse, delivered to a thousand former fire-worshippers at Gayāsīsa. "All is burning" — the six sense bases, their objects, contact, feeling — burning with the fires of greed, hatred, and delusion. The disenchantment that frees the mind.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/sensual_craving|Sensual craving]] — When sensual pleasures are pulling at the mind. The suttas that show craving's mechanism and its cost — not moralizing, but clear sight.
