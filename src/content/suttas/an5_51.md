# AN 5.51 — Āvaraṇa Sutta
*The Five Obstructions*

The five hindrances as obstructions to wisdom — like dirty water in which one cannot see one's own reflection. Naming them is the first step in their removal.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/hindrances|The five hindrances]] — The specific five things that obstruct concentration and clarity — named, diagnosed, and given antidotes. Required reading for anyone sitting regularly.
