# MN 58 — Abhayarājakumāra Sutta
*To Prince Abhaya*

Prince Abhaya asks the Buddha how he speaks. The Buddha answers with a specific six-fold test: is it true? is it beneficial? is it welcome? is the time right? — and applies it across the possibilities. The canonical framework for skillful speech, simpler than it first appears and hard to actually live.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/speech|How to speak]] — What to say, how to say it, and when silence is kinder. The Buddha was specific: true, beneficial, timely, spoken with a mind of good-will.
