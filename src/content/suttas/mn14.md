# MN 14 — Cūladukkhakkhandha Sutta
*The Shorter Discourse on the Mass of Suffering*

The drawback of sensual pleasures examined directly: the gratification, the danger, and the escape. The pleasure is small; the suffering it generates is great.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/sensual_craving|Sensual craving]] — When sensual pleasures are pulling at the mind. The suttas that show craving's mechanism and its cost — not moralizing, but clear sight.
