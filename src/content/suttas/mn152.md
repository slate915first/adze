# MN 152 — Indriyabhāvanā Sutta
*The Development of the Faculties*

The supreme development of the sense faculties — not avoiding contact but training the response to it. The advanced practice that takes the practitioner beyond mere hindrance management.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/meditation_method|How to meditate]] — The Buddha's own meditation instructions. Not technique A vs technique B — the complete curriculum, from the breath to the six sense bases.
