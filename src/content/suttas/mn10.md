# MN 10 — Satipaṭṭhāna Sutta
*Foundations of Mindfulness*

The Buddha's direct teaching on the four foundations of mindfulness — body, feeling tone, mind, and phenomena. The complete framework for vipassanā.

## When to return to this sutta
