# MN 9 — Sammādiṭṭhi Sutta
*Right View*

Sāriputta's classical exposition of right view — the wholesome and unwholesome, nutriment, the Four Noble Truths, dependent origination, the taints. The sutta almost everyone points to for understanding what "right view" actually means.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/right_view|Right view]] — The forerunner of the entire path. Not a belief — a way of seeing. Several complementary doors into it, from several teachers.
