# Sn 2.4 — Maṅgala Sutta
*The Blessings*

The Buddha answers a deva who asks what is the greatest blessing — and enumerates thirty-eight, from avoiding fools and honoring the worthy through to touching nibbāna. A complete map of the graded practice for a lay practitioner.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/family_and_friends|Family & friendship]] — How a householder relates to parents, spouse, children, friends. The Buddha taught a specific code for lay life; it is still useful.
- [[subcategories/livelihood|Right livelihood]] — Work that does not harm. What counts as wrong livelihood. The balance between making a living and staying honest.
