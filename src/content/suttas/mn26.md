# MN 26 — Ariyapariyesana Sutta
*The Noble Search*

The Buddha's own account of his going forth, his training with Āḷāra Kālāma and Uddaka Rāmaputta, and the moment he walked away from their teachings recognizing they stopped short of liberation. The sutta the vow of the path is drawn from.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/going_forth|The search & awakening]] — The Bodhisatta's own journey — from the going-forth to the awakening to the final nibbāna. The arc the whole path is walking.
