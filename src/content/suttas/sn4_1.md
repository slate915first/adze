# SN 4.1 — Tapokamma Sutta
*Self-Mortification*

Māra appears to the Buddha just after his awakening to discourage him from teaching. The first of many post-awakening encounters showing Māra still visits but cannot find purchase.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/effort|Effort & perseverance]] — When you want to give up. When the practice feels stuck. The Bodhisatta's own encounters with Māra — and how he stayed.
