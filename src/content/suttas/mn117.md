# MN 117 — Mahācattārīsaka Sutta
*The Great Forty*

Right view explained as the forerunner of the entire path. Each path-factor is shown in two modes — mundane and supramundane — and the interdependence of all eight factors is laid out. The reference sutta for the eightfold path as a single integrated whole.

## When to return to this sutta
- [[subcategories/right_view|Right view]] — The forerunner of the entire path. Not a belief — a way of seeing. Several complementary doors into it, from several teachers.
- [[subcategories/livelihood|Right livelihood]] — Work that does not harm. What counts as wrong livelihood. The balance between making a living and staying honest.
- [[subcategories/speech|How to speak]] — What to say, how to say it, and when silence is kinder. The Buddha was specific: true, beneficial, timely, spoken with a mind of good-will.
